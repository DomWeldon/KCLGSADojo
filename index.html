Why are we here?

Tonight is about:

  • Having a fun time
  • Practicing skills and learning from eachother
  • Seeing uses of code outside geography
  • Having a laugh as geographers
  • Meeting new coursemates!

Pairing Up

In groups of 2 or 3, experienced/confident coders to pair with newer pythonistas. Form groups with people you don't know well or haven't worked with before.

What's the challenge?

Using only PyGame Zero and the standard library, create an animation and/or game to demonstrate some concept in geography.

Top Tips:

  • Don't over-think it!
  • Have fun! (make us laugh?)
  • Don't worry when things go wrong!
  • Learn from eachother in your groups

When you're done, share your work on the #dojo channel in Slack, so we can put it up to present.
